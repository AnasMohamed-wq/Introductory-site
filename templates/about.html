Who We Are

Shalash Al-Sulaiman Trading and Contracting is a premier engineering services provider, established in 2011 to bridge the gap between complex industrial requirements and sophisticated technical execution.

We specialize in multi-disciplinary engineering services with a particular focus on high-stakes sectors like oil and gas infrastructure. Our reputation is built on a foundation of technical excellence, rigorous safety standards, and a commitment to delivering projects that stand the test of time.
